Double quotes, only and always

For ALL tag properties, you have to use double quotes. Even if you are passing a variable as a parameter, the double quotes are mandatory.

What follows will be parsed incorrectly: 


<fs:input type="text" name=foo />

The error you will get depends on the the attribute that does not have the double quotes. If it's a mandatory one, you will get a warning; if it's an optionnal one, you might get weird results, or your property might simply get ignored.
